About Us


The call to action for my audience differs from group to group. For those who are new soccer fans, I want them to go out there and pick a team or two to support and talk to their community. For those who are dedicated fans, I want them to watch and share their thoughts in order to grow the community even more. However, no matter what type of fan they are, I want them to be able to support their teams as much as possible, through watching games and cheering them on.
